Okazii.ro is the biggest online store in Romania. You can buy anything from here - from smart- phones to fishing rods, tyres, cars and even os- trich eggs. The concept of this campaign is to show what would happen if Okazii.ro made a tv commercial: what products would they include, since the variety is so overwhelming? A long tracking shot takes the spectator through diffe- rent sets, filled with a variety of products.
