Jukebox Collective is an interactive platform that allows pub customers to connect with each other through playing music according to a given theme (lost in the forest, morning routine etc). This way you can play your music in the pub individually or as a group and interact with other customers through the gamification elements of the app.

Etnographical research insights:
Antic Pub customers are nostalgic about the times when they used to walk into a pub and start chat- ting with new people. Also, the pub is famous for its music.
