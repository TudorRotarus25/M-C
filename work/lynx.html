#NotAGod is a propaganda film for Lynx that breaks the stereotypes that boys and young men see in the media and feel pressured to comply with. In this campaign we reverse the approach that the brand previously had: we start from an obviously fake yet funny premise - that there is a Sex God, which addresses directly men insecurities in relation to girls.

To activate this campaign on social medias we created the Sex God fake Tinder profil, build with funny GIFs of him showcasing his godly look, the bio describes him and invites users to see a short film about him. Tinder was the social plat- form on point to talk about sex and love among the young generation. This fake profile repre- sented well his fake godly aura and users would be motivated to share this funny trickery on other social networks (Facebook, twitter etc.).
