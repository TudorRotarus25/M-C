It’s movie night! For a cinephile, what movie to watch can be a paramount decision, especially tonight when he wants to impress his date. He places such importance on this problem that the world speaks to him in film references and he can’t tell reality from his imagination anymore. Should it be Tarantino dialogues? Hitchcock suspense? Luc Besson cinema du look? or Stanley Kubrick terrifying composition? He is completely overwhelmed by choices and terrified of the wrong decision. After an exhausting journey through cinema references, the girl comes with a solution: with Mubi, each choice is the right choice.
